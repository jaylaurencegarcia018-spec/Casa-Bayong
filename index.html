Our Story

Crafted from the
Heart of the Philippines

Casa Bayong was born from a single belief: that the humble bayong — the traditional Filipino woven bag — deserves its place on the world stage.

Experience the charm of handcrafted artistry with our DIY painted native bags, thoughtfully made and designed with love. Each piece is uniquely hand-painted, making no two bags exactly the same. When you choose our products, you’re not just purchasing a bag, you’re supporting local creativity, culture, and passion. Perfect for daily use or as a special gift, our bags bring a distinctive and artistic touch to your style. Join us from the very beginning of our journey and carry something truly unique with you.

Sustainable

All-natural, biodegradable materials from responsibly managed sources.

Handcrafted

Zero machines. Every stitch and stroke is done by skilled human hands.

Community-First

Fairly compensating over 50 artisan families across the Philippines.
